<p><p>The Ash Conveyor Market encompasses various types designed for efficient ash handling in industrial applications. Drag Chain Conveyors utilize chains to transport ash within a trough, ensuring reliability and low maintenance. Screw Conveyors move ash through a helical screw mechanism, ideal for densely packed materials. Submerged Drag Conveyors operate below the surface of ash, minimizing dust and enhancing safety. Other market types include bucket elevators and belt conveyors, each tailored to specific operational requirements in ash disposal and management.</p></p>
