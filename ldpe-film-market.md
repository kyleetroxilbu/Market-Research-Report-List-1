<p><p>The LDPE film market is primarily divided into two types: General LDPE film and Metalized LDPE film. General LDPE film is widely used for packaging applications due to its flexibility, moisture resistance, and affordability, making it ideal for items like bags and wraps. In contrast, Metalized LDPE film incorporates a thin layer of metal, enhancing barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light. This type is often utilized for high-end packaging solutions, providing improved shelf life and product protection.</p></p>
