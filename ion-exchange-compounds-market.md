<p><p>The ion exchange compounds market is categorized into two main types: anion exchange compounds and cation exchange compounds. Anion exchange compounds facilitate the removal of negatively charged ions from solutions, making them essential in water treatment and chemical processing. Conversely, cation exchange compounds target positively charged ions, playing a crucial role in softening water and removing heavy metals. Both types are pivotal in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and environmental management, driving demand for efficient and sustainable ion exchange technologies.</p></p>
