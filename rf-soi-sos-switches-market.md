<p><p>RF SOI (Radio Frequency Silicon-On-Insulator) switches and RF SOS (Silicon On Sapphire) switches are specialized components used in communication systems. RF SOI switches leverage a silicon-on-insulator substrate to enhance performance in RF applications, offering low power consumption and high linearity. In contrast, RF SOS switches utilize a sapphire substrate, providing excellent thermal stability and isolation, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Together, these switch types cater to diverse markets, including mobile communications, aerospace, and consumer electronics, driving advancements in RF technology.</p></p>
