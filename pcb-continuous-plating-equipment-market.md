<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB Continuous Plating Equipment market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical configurations. Horizontal plating equipment features a flat conveyor system that allows PCBs to pass through various plating processes in a continuous flow, promoting efficient production and uniform coating. Vertical plating equipment, on the other hand, utilizes a vertical setup where PCBs are immersed in plating solutions, typically allowing for space-saving designs and improved accessibility. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs in the growing PCB industry.</p></p>
