<p><p>The Helicobacter Pylori testing kit market comprises various testing types, primarily antibody tests and antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies in the blood, indicating past or present infection. In contrast, antigen tests identify H. pylori proteins in stool samples, providing more immediate results. Both methods serve to diagnose infections linked to gastrointestinal disorders. The choice between these tests often depends on factors like accuracy, convenience, time sensitivity, and patient-specific considerations.</p></p>
