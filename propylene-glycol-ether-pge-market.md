<p><p>The Propylene Glycol Ether (PGE) market comprises three main types: PM (Propylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether), DPM (Dipropylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether), and TPM (Tripropylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether). PM is widely used as a solvent in coatings and cleaning products, while DPM offers enhanced performance properties for applications such as paints and inks. TPM, with its higher molecular weight, is suitable for specialized applications requiring lower volatility and greater solvency. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
